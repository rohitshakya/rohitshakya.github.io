SmartShip is a one-stop logistics solution designed to make shipping **Easier, Faster, and Cheaper** for businesses across India.  
It provides merchants with seamless access to the **largest pincode network**, **best courier rates**, and **real-time tracking** through a robust and simple technology platform.

SmartShip integrates multiple courier partners and automates every step from order creation to label generation, ensuring optimized shipping performance and reduced logistics costs.

You can explore more at **smartship.in**.

---

## Key Features

- **Largest Pincode Network** across India  
- **Best Price Algorithm** for selecting the most cost-effective courier  
- **Simple, clean & comprehensive merchant dashboard**  
- **Real-time shipment tracking & status updates**  
- **Automated courier allocation** based on SLA, cost, and serviceability  
- **Optimized label generation pipeline**  
- **COD, prepaid, return, and exchange workflows**  
- **Bulk order import and API integrations**  
- **Daily CRON-based jobs** for reconciliation, tracking sync, and performance reports  

---

## Technology Stack

- **Backend:** PHP, SLIM Framework  
- **Architecture:** Dependency Injection, Modular Courier Drivers  
- **Database:** MySQL with optimized indexing for large shipment datasets  
- **Automation:** CRON Jobs for tracking, billing, reconciliation  
- **Integrations:** Multi-courier APIs (Delhivery, DTDC, Ecom Express, BlueDart, XpressBees, etc.)  
- **Algorithms:** Smart courier recommendation with price, SLA & serviceability ranking
